Oba to J'oba Lo (3X)
(The KING above all kings)
 
Your Excellency KABIYESI O ...OBA to J'oba lo
(Your KING Excellency. KING of above all Kings)
 
Your Supremacy KABIYESI O ...OBA to J'oba lo
(Your KING Supremacy. KING of above all Kings)
 
Forget about yourself let us Worship the LORD
Forget about yourself let us praise the LORD
 
HE is the KING of Kings.
HE is the LORD of Lords
 
We call HIM Kabiyesi o. OBA to j'Oba lo
(We call HIM KING. KING above all Kings)
